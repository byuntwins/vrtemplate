Medical Images to VR 3D Models

View and manually control the accurate, 3D models from the patient

About

This project aims to allow the physician and the patient to interact on a virtual reality platform with 3D models created from the patient's CT/MRI scans.

This will drastically ease the delivery of information from the physician to the patient, guaranteeing the improvement in both the patient care and the patient experience.

Process

Obtain patient images

Conversion through volumetric rendering

Patient education through an immersive experience

Impact

This immersive experience will redefine the very meaning of patient - physician interaction in modern medicine.
